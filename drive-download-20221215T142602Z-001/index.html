2K League Teams

When the 2K League intially kicked off in 2018 there was 17 teams in total. Since then, 8 teams has been added to the league roster. 22 of the 25 teams are affliated with the Official NBA League, while the rest of the teams were created purely for the 2K League. Each team is composed of 5 players which are selected through a three round draft process.

Perfomance of the Week

Each week, a player of the week will be selected based off of performance. Performance of the week is purely opinion based and it will go to the player who had benefited his team the most. All games played in the week, Sunday through Saturday, will be accounted for.
