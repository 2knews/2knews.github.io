The League in a Whole

The League is not just ran by the NBA League, although assisted, it is mainly run by Two Take Interactive. The 2K League is the first officil esports league ran and operated by a U.S professional sports league. The first season of the 2K league took place in 2017 and and started with just 17 teams, since then it has grown to over 24 teams. 22 of these teams are affliated with NBA teams while the rest are rans by 2k league coaches and players. Finally, 2k league players play in 5 v 5 and 3 v 3 tournament using custom players used to fit a certain postion.
