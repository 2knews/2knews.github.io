The Updates

Tournament-Centric Season Structure

The 2K season used to be a normal "regular season" but now they are switch to a tournament type season to allow for money to be won, there will also be events through out the season in order to qualify for these tournaments. There will still be a playoffs and qualifying for the playoffs is based off the tournament performance.

New 3v3 Tournaments

The 2K League is deciding to add a 3v3 Tournament to allow for more people to connect with the league players because 3v3 is the most played court of all. Each 2K League team consists of five players, the team captain will choose the three best fit players to compete in the tournament.

A Lot Higher Prize Pool

The entire prize pool, which includes the pool for the traditional 5v5 competition and for the brand-new 3v3 competitions, will be much more than the total prize pool for any previous NBA 2K League season. In the upcoming months, additional details about the prize pool will be released.

Amateur Teams vs. NBA 2K League Teams

With NBA 2K League teams, amateur teams from all over the world will get the chance to compete for a part of the 3v3 tournament prize money. The 24 NBA 2K League teams and amateur teams from North America will compete in the first two of these 3v3 competitions. Amateur teams from North America will be able to participate in games to secure slots in these first two 3v3 championships' group play phases.
